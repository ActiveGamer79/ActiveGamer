Game Overview

Minecraft is a sandbox video game where players explore a blocky, procedurally generated 3D world with infinite terrain, and may discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and simple machines. This package includes both the Java Edition (known for mods and deep customization) and the Bedrock Edition (known for cross-platform play). Unleash your creativity!
